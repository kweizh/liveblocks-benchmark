3. **Smooth Follow Tracking**: When an avatar is clicked, `data-following` updates to that user's `connectionId`. The canvas viewport smoothly translates to center the followed user's last known cursor position in the viewport (computed as `Math.round(cursor - windowSize / 2)`). 4. **Release Follow Mode**: Clicking the same avatar a second time or clicking anywhere else on the document (including clicking or dragging the canvas) immediately stops following and leaves the viewport at its current offset.
5. **Pannable Canvas**: Users can manually pan around the `4000 x 4000` canvas by dragging. During active dragging, the CSS `transition` is temporarily disabled (`transition: none`) to ensure perfectly responsive, low-latency movement.
6. **Attributes & State Expose**:
